SCC v/s MSEB

PDCA Sadu Shinde League (45 overs)

2nd Match : Date - 23/11/14 : Venue : NCL

Having lost first match SCC was determined to make amends. Winning the toss MSEB elected to Bat.

Ravi started with bang having Nitin & Akshay early in MSEB innings. Nikit followed up by trapping Sandeep LBW & Paresh with a beautiful in swinger. Bowling change did not help MSEB as Akhilesh was stumped in air in a flash by Sudhakar.. Score reading 5-38 .

Nikit continued his rhythm & bagged 4 wickets 32 runs earning him MOM award in 9 overs.

Chetan, Sandeep & Yuvraj cleaned the bottom half swiftly to bundle MSEB for 68 .

With a small gettable total SCC had very bad start with Amol falling for caught by a beauty of Akhilesh & in next over Sudhakar was unnecessary run out not soon before Nikit falling cheaply.

This prompted to send Ravi in top order but was jolted by pace of Akhilesh & was cleaned up . Subash followed soon & SCC score read 5-30. This prompted Dhan to ask Satish who has an injured figure to Join him and bring some stability in the batting.

Dhan & Satish played on the merit of the balls & slowly constructed the innings & when the target was in the reach, both played some attacking cricketing shots to guide SCC to 5 wickets victory and scoring the desired runs in 14 overs (RR- 5/over) to get full batting points !

Dhan & Satish remained unbeaten on 21 off 30 balls & 18 off 17 balls respectively to bring cheer to SCC camp.
