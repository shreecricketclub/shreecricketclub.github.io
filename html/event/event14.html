SCC v/s Sinhagad

at Dighi Date : 24/11/13 PDCA 45 Overs 2nd Match :

Match was played at Spiritual locality of Alandi ...This was the freshly laid wicket and ground was conducting the first PDCA match. The view was scenic with hills at background ....

Sinhagad won the toss and elected to Bat . Aggressive Batting by openers and wayward new ball spell needed heroics of Dhan throw to run out Matale.

Then another freak dismissal of Arvind for 33 runs boosted morale of SCC. Sandeep chipped in with a wicket each till Ramesh & Sandesh stitched a good partnership of 50 runs followed by Chirag (27) who played a good supporting hand to Ramesh & added another 65 runs till Dhan broke the partnership.

Ultimately Ramesh was out in order to step up scoring rate for well made 84 runs .

The debutant Chetan bowled well for his 2-22 in his 6 over spell.

Target was set for 227 runs in 45 overs.

SCC started usually by loosing 1st wicket in first over. Abhisheek the other debutant from SCC hung out & made pain-sticking 10 runs in 50 balls. Sandeep also contribute with his 16 runs. While Rohan bowled good pace, Monish bowled tidy & gave only 14 runs in his 9 over spell ! SCC kept loosing wickets till Ravi got support from Sudhakar. The latter half showed the positiveness & scored some fluent boundaries, but as happens Sudhakar(18) was out when hopes were raised.

Subhash who played well in his previous match continued his form & the partnership blossomed with 80 runs needed in 14 overs. But smart run outs of Subhash ( 20 ) & Harish closed the match for Sinhgad.

Ravikumar was out last man for well made 52 runs.

SCC lost by 67 runs.
