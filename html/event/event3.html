SCC v/s Champion

at SSPMS PDCA 45OVERS 1st Match

A good start with lost...

Dear all we had our first match exactly after one year,

It was with Champion Cricket Club. We won the toss & decided to Bat first.

Our Batting collapsed against Superb bowling from opponent side but SCC managed to reach 100.

Defending the such a small target was big challenge, Ravi draw the first blood in his 2nd over but later were not baler to break through and we conceeded lot of wides . Later Sadeep took one wicket but we were leaking runs . Then Harish 3 wicket spell in a over brought us back in game . Wasim supported with two wickets. SCC fought hard but finally the Champion managed to reach the target with the fall of 7 wickets.

But for Club its Good news that Nikit bag 7 wickets vs Rajashtan in under25 Ranaji Match at Jaipur !
