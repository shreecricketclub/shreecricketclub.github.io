SCC Vs Minty Cricket Club

PDCA T20 1st Match 2014

Dt : 23/03/14

After tasting two successive victories SCC were on roll. Toss was won by SCC and without hesitation opted to bowl.

MCC quickly lost both the openers to good bowling of Ravi & Yuvraj in bowlers first over. Ravi holding a slash at point of Rahul . In a matter of time MCC were reduced to 40 for loss of half of their side. Only Nikhil & Suhas went past 20. Dhan chipped in with two wickets in between while Sandeep accounted for one.

MCC were bundled for modest 106 in 20 overs and SCC gained full bowling points.

SCC opened with bang with in form Amol playing freely with Abhishek taking score past 50 ! Abhishek made polished 20 (3X4) & Amol nice 37 (4X4)before being bowled by Makarand.

To score quickly & gain some points Captain sent in Ravi & he literally murdered MCC bowlers with 32 not out hitting 4 boundaries & a huge six off 15 balls to become Batsman of the Match !

SCC won in 16th over by 8 wickets and maintained its supremacy against MCC.

For economical bowling & 3 wickets Yuvraj was bowler of the match.

It was good team effort & supported by good fielding
