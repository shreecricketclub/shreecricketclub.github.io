SCC Vs Bank of Baroda

PDCA T20 3rd Match 2014 Dt : 20/04/14

Venue : Dighi

On a hot morning BOB opted to Bowl first considering afternoon temperature & SCC having successfully chased Scores on four previous occasion. ( Twice in 45 over & in T 20).

The start was sedate & steady for SCC. By 10th over Score was reading 55 with loss of Chetan & Satish wickets. Amol was plucky and Lucky before he was out for 36 with a four & huge Straight six.

But arrival of Ravi 19 runs ( 4X3) & then Sudhakar 27 (4x3) brought some excitement & respect to SCC Score but not before SCC losing 8 wickets for 127 runs in allocated 20 overs. The fact that outfield was big & slow SCC reached a fighting total.

Nikit started with a good maiden over. Sandip picked up a wicket in fourth over of the innings. Changing of bowlers in quick succession did not help BOB to settle & in Satish 2nd over BOB lost another wicket .

Ravi & Satish bowled in tandem and snared two wickets each to keep BOB in check. However, Shashank was in severe mood and hit 4 boundaries & a six off Ravi to give SCC worth a scare.

Chetan bowled a good ball to Shashank to drive & in process lost his balance & Sudhakar did the rest in a flash to take air out of BOB chase. Nikit & Chetan did not give any further room to BOB & competed their spells

economically to give SCC win by 16 runs.

This was 5th consecutive victory with team effort for SCC.…But the price was paid with the injury of Captain Dhan who while diving to take a catch in the slips hurt his shoulder.

The injury will keep Dhan out for few weeks ….

Team is expected to perform in similar fashion for rest of the season…Hip Hip Hurray ! ! !
