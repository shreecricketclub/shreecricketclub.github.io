SCC vs Unity Council

SCC crushed the Unity Council

After tasting hard fought victory in their last match, SCC were eager to improve on their performance.

Unity Council won the toss & elected to bat on Sunday afternoon.

SCC opened the attack with Ravi & Amol. It was a unique combination of Fast left Arm with Medium pace Right Arm Bowling. Opening Batsmen were too cautious. Batsmen were unable to play the cross pitching fast ones of Ravi while Amol kept his length tidy with his unusual action and got 2 wickets.. Pitch was a bit on slower side.

SCC enforced spin duo of Sandeep & Wasim and were rewarded handsomely. Batsmen did not take initiative and bowlers were all over them.

Though SCC bowled well but could not bowl Unity out. Unity managed to score 81 runs in allocated 20 overs while losing 6 wickets.

It was very easy task & SCC decided to score in 11 over to gain Bonus Point. Opening Duo Wasim & Sumedh looks very confident with both hit very good straight drive to boundary. At one stage SCC were 30 in 4 overs then suddenly lost way as a few wickets fell. The brakes were put on the scoring rate.

After some confusion & push from pavilion SCC scratched their way by getting to target in 13 overs salvaging some batting points only after Ravi entered the scene.

A special mention for the sharp wicket keeping by SCC Sudhakar !

It was an easy win for SCC. Now SCC needs to keep the winning momentum.

BEST PERFORMER OF THE DAY: - SANDEEP, SUMEDH & AMOL.
