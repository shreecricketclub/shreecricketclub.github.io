SCC v/s Evergreen

PDCA Sadu Shinde League (45 overs)

2rd Match : Date - 21/12/14 : Venue : D Y Patil (Salundre Gaon)

On a chilly morning driving 30 kms away from Pune towards Mumbai was an experience itself. Cars were parked away as the approach road was not good. But once at the ground it was a pretty sight .

The green grass cover was welcome sight. SCC decided to Bat first considering dew will have problems for bowling side.

Prasad made debut for SCC

SCC begun well with 37 runs for the opening wicket but soon the openers were gone. Amol hit sound 23 runs with 4 crisp boundaries. Sandeep played well but could not find support, He made 21 runs with 3 hits to fence. But score board read pathetic 7-93.Then the rear guard action by Ravi & Sudhakar caused concern in Evergreen camp. The left & right hand combination played for 14 overs and stitched entertaining partnership of 78 runs. Sudhakar got out for tired shot for his 33 runs with 3 fours & Ravi missed out his well-deserved half century by a run . Ravi made 49 runs with 3 fours & 2 big sixes.

SCC were all out for 185 with 5 overs left.

ECC began cautiously knowing the run rate is within reach . But soon got in to trouble with 3/43 with Sandeep , Ravi & Yuvi snaring a wicket each. That brought Saurav & Ruchir two Under 19 players of Maharashtra combining well to grind SCC bowlers making 92 runs in 20 overs to take score to 135.

Saurav scored 53 ( 74 ) with 5 fours & Ruchir made 44 ( 62 ) with 6 boundaries. Both went in quick succession when Harish was introduced to attack. But Abhijeet & Jadhav too played out SCC bowlers Ravi, Amol, Yuvi, Sandeep, Harish who bowled well but without getting breakthrough at regular intervals.

Finally Evergreen overhauled SCC score at 41.3 overs with 3 wickets in hand.

Harish figures were 3/17 while Sandeep 2/44 .

A good match SCC lost against good young team.
