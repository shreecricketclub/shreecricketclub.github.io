Mother Teressa Home

As it is said .... Be faithful in small things because it is in them that your strength lies. We organized a second outdoor visit of Old / Destitute /orphaned in mates of Mother Teressa Home.

Pune to Rajiv Gandhi Zoo on 22nd Sept 12. The earlier visit was for the Circus near Deccan . Being unwanted, unloved, uncared for, forgotten by everybody, I think that is a much greater hunger, a much greater poverty than the person who has nothing to eat.

So , we spent a day with them and seeing them happy brought joy to us. We plan to spread this message and thank god for all that has been given to us and really we are very privileged.
