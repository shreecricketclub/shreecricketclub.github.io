A chrismas gift - our way

God has been great to provide us repeated opportunity to celebrate Christmas & eve of New Year 2015 with inmates of Mother Teresa Destitute Home

Many of the inmates had shared desire to travel by train & have fun during journey like normal people.

So, on 27thDecember, We {Satish, Dhananjay & Shikhar ( s/o Satish ) } reached the Mother Teresa home at 8 am on chilly morning. 28 inmates along with the Sister got in to the Train with our help. The joy of the journey was to be seen on all faces. In no time all started signing, playing and enjoying the outside scenery it gave other passengers to join & clap with them. The energy was extraordinary and their joy unparalleled.

The train took an hour to reach Talegaon. There all had nice puffins / cake /Juice. The return journey was equally enjoyable with all of us joining them in singing..Some wanted to stand near the window to see the river / bridge and stations.

Once back all the inmates were thanking us from bottom of their heart and there shaking hand with us and face gave us immense satisfaction & encouragement to carry on with our small attempt in bringing joy to others..

Special mention to teenage Shikhar- for his involvement in time sharing experience.

Look forward for next opportunity …………..

Good bye 2014.
