Welcome to Shree Cricket Club

What is SCC ??

It is a private club consortium. The sport of cricket is the thread which binds its members from different walks of life.

From a small and simple game of cricket played by a small young and enthusiastic cricketers at different grounds depending upon the availability way back in 1998 this developed in to a regular habit of keen and enthused cricketers who shared similar values with varied background to form true “Shree Cricket Club “.

Now this humble group is all set to meet the growing demands of society by vital contribution towards the promotion of sportsmanship spirit and as a social club for helping under privileged, health and society in general.

Shree cricket Club, Now well known as SCC is now refurbishing its web site and has undertaken an ambitious plan to set up a group of like minded individuals coming from different educational, religious & professional

background who want to contribute and share their experiences for the development of society and help the local administration in event of any emergency situation.

The history of SCC would be incomplete without mentioning the contribution made by Mr.Makarand Bhide who toiled hard to keep the flame alive of this club. Mr. Bhide has been participating and is associated with P.D.C.A for over 39 years. He has received .certificates for Batting , bowling & fielding from PDCA He has climbed Sinhgarh Fort in record 23 minutes and has to his credit being the fastest to climb Parvati hills in Pune for 3 consecutive years! He is an enthusiastic cyclist too at this age of 55 years.

We Believe In…

  1. Accepting people as they are, not as we would like them to be.
  2. Approaching relationships and problems in terms of the present rather than the past.
  3. Treating those who are close to us with the same courteous attention that we extend to our family.
  4. Trusting others, even if the risk seems great.
  5. Working without constant approval and recognition from others.
  • Leadership...

    We believe in leading people by walking besides them.

  • Recognition...

    We believe in recognizing the good qualities.

  • Team Working…

    We believe in working as a family than a Club.

  • Relationships...

    We believe in building & fostering relationships which last till eternity

We are confident that we would be able to complete our cherished dream into reality very soon with the blessings and support of our existing, new and prospective members. During these past 15years, SCC members have made a significant contribution towards the social issues like participating in blood donation camps , being member of education groups for under privileged ,etc.

The club has number of players who have achieved much success in their personal & professional careers.
